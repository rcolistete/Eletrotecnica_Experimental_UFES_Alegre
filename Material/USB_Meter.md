## (Rui Deng) RD UM25

<img src="https://www.back2gaming.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/UM25-USB-METER-Interfaces.jpg" alt="RD UM25C" style="zoom: 67%;" />

Azul, sem Bluetooth (UM25C tem Bluetooth).

- USB 2.0;
- entrada USB-A, micro-USB e USB-C, saída USB-A e USB-C;
- 1.44" color LCD display;
- 4.000-24.000 V, resolution 0.001 V, accuracy ± (0.5％ + 2 digits);
- 0.0000-3.0000 A, resolution 0.0001 A, accuracy ± (1％ + 4 digits);
- load impedance 0.8Ω-9999.9Ω
- 0-99999 mAh;
- 0-99999 mWh;
- protocolos QC2.0, QC3.0, Apple 2.4A/2.1A/1A/0.5A, Android DCP, Samsung.
